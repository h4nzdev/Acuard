# **App Name**: AcademiaGuard

## Core Features:

- Secure User Authentication: Allow instructors and students to securely log in to their respective portals, protecting sensitive academic data.
- Student Writing Baseline & Fingerprint Tool: Students complete a baseline writing assessment to capture unique typing and writing style patterns, which a tool uses to form a 'writing fingerprint' for future comparison.
- Real-time Assessment Behavior Monitoring: During online assessments, the system continuously tracks student behavior including typing speed, copy-paste events, and tab switching activities.
- Configurable Copy-Paste Policy Tool: A tool for instructors to define and apply specific copy-paste rules for each assessment activity (e.g., disallowed, monitored, fully allowed), ensuring fairness.
- Predictive Academic Integrity Risk Tool: A machine learning tool that processes monitored student behavior data and compares it against the student's writing fingerprint to generate a real-time academic integrity risk score (Normal, Suspicious, Highly Suspicious) for instructors.
- Instructor Monitoring Dashboard: Provide instructors with a real-time overview of all active assessments, displaying student statuses, accumulated warning counts, and ML-generated integrity risk scores.
- Student Honesty Tracker: Students can view their personal 'honesty score', total warning counts, and a chronological history of their assessment activities.

## Style Guidelines:

- The chosen color palette evokes trust, analytical rigor, and an educational environment. The primary color, a deep, professional blue (#335599), conveys authority and clarity. It is well-suited for a light scheme as it provides sufficient contrast. This choice reflects the app's serious purpose in maintaining academic integrity without being overly stark.
- The background color is a heavily desaturated blue (#F0F3F8), maintaining a light, clean aesthetic that minimizes eye strain and ensures content readability in a professional setting. Its subtle blue hue ties back to the primary color, providing cohesion.
- The accent color is a vibrant violet (#6026D9). Positioned approximately 30 degrees clockwise from the primary hue on the color wheel, it creates a striking but harmonious contrast. This color will be used sparingly for calls-to-action, warnings, and highlighting key data points, drawing attention to critical information effectively.
- Headlines will utilize 'Alegreya', a humanist serif font. Its elegant and intellectual feel is suitable for conveying the seriousness and scholarly nature of academic integrity. Its contemporary style provides an inviting yet formal touch to prominent text.
- Body text will use 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font. Its modern, neutral, and highly readable design ensures clarity for detailed reports, policies, and instructional content, supporting prolonged reading without fatigue. This pairing creates a balanced visual hierarchy, enhancing readability and user experience.
- Utilize minimalist line icons that represent actions, statuses, and data categories (e.g., a shield for integrity, an eye for monitoring, a checkmark for pass/fail, a caution triangle for warnings) to maintain a clean and professional aesthetic.
- Adopt a clean, data-driven layout with clearly defined sections and hierarchical information display on dashboards. Utilize cards and tables for structured presentation of student data and assessment monitoring. Employ responsive design principles for optimal viewing across various devices, particularly prioritizing desktop access for instructors.
- Implement subtle transition animations for loading states on dashboards and during assessment submission to provide visual feedback and improve perceived responsiveness without distracting from critical information.
